Frequently Asked Questions

How are the Ganpati Idols shipped?
All our Ganpati idols are packed using 5-layer heavy corrugated boxes, bubble wraps, and thermocol corner guards to ensure 100% safe transit. We partner with reliable courier services like DHL and FedEx to ensure timely delivery.
Are the Ganpati Idols completely eco-friendly?
Yes! Our eco-friendly Ganpati idols are handcrafted using pure Shadu clay (natural river silt) and painted using natural water-soluble colors. They dissolve completely in water in less than 45 minutes during visarjan.
Can I request customization for handmade gifts?
Absolutely. We support custom name tags, personalized message cards, and color choices for our decorative trays and gift sets. Please get in touch with us via our contact form or WhatsApp immediately after placing your order.
What is the return/refund policy?
Due to the fragile and handmade nature of our crafts, we do not accept returns. However, in the rare event that a product arrives damaged in transit, we will send an immediate replacement or issue a full refund upon receiving opening video proof within 24 hours of delivery.
